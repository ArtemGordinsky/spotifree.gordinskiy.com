Troubleshooting

Spotifree gives you a headache?

Spotifree doesn't work? Try these:

    1. Reinstall Spotify using the official instructions. No worries, all your music will be there when you're done — it's stored in the cloud.

    2. Open Spotify, and sign in with Facebook or your own credentials.

    1. Terminate Spotifree process (if any) using the Activity Monitor (Applications → Utilities → Activity Monitor);

    2. Get a fresh app from the homepage. Install it, replacing the old one.

    3. Run the app again.

If you have followed both of these instructions and your problem is still there, feel free to send me a message.
